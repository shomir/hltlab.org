Openings

HLT Lab periodically has openings for Ph.D., M.S., and undergraduate students to work on projects related to natural language processing, NLP applications, or privacy. Applicants should possess strong writing skills and an attentiveness to details. Prospective graduate students should have a strong background in computer science, information science, computational linguistics, or a related discipline. Coursework in NLP, artificial intelligence, or machine learning is desirable. Please see Dr. Wilson's website for more information and instructions on getting in touch. Prospective Ph.D. and M.S. students should also consult the College of IST's application requirements.

HLT Lab currently does not have openings for short term (e.g., single semester or summer length) "interns" from other universities.
