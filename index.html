About Us

Natural language is crucial to how humanity communicates, synthesizes, and stores information. Our lab solves problems to enable computers to do meaningful work with large volumes of text. We develop methods for natural language processing (NLP) and apply them to a variety of domains, including privacy, online social networks, web science, and digital libraries.

Our lab is part of the College of Information Sciences and Technology at Penn State University.

Research Artifacts

For lab news and publications, see the website of lab director Prof. Wilson.

Opt-Out Easy is a web browser plugin that helps internet users take control of their privacy. It is a product of our collaboration with researchers at Carnegie Mellon University, Stanford University, and the University of Michigan. You can read more about it in this press release.

You can also check out PrivaSeer, our search engine for website privacy policies.
