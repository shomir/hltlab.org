The Human Language Technologies Lab at Penn State

About Us

Natural language is crucial to how humanity communicates, synthesizes, and stores information. Our lab solves problems to enable computers to do meaningful work with large volumes of natural language text. We apply methods from natural language processing (NLP) in a variety of domains, including privacy, online social networks, web science, and digital libraries.

Since August 2018, the lab has been located in the College of Information Sciences and Technology at Penn State. Prior to that it was in the EECS Department at the University of Cincinnati.
